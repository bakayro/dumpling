Hello my Friends,

My name is Aleksandra, but friends and family call me Ola. Cooking was always my huge passion. I’ve became vegan 5 years ago I’ve started my sharing vegan recipes on my blog eatntravelwithola.pl. My goal is to show you that vegan lifestyle can be a wonderful journey and help you stay healthy. I want to inspire you to embrace more plant-based foods in your diet without feeling the least bit deprived. You’ll find recipes that are focused on both pleasure and wellness here.

I love experiment in the kitchen and creating new, fun and healthy recipes. My other biggest passion is travel which I am sharing with you on my BLOG section. I hope you will jump into this fantastic journey with me.
